
Task 1: Data Cleaning & Preprocessing Summary

Objective:
Clean and prepare the raw Netflix titles dataset for further analysis.

Steps Performed:

1. Duplicate Handling
- Identified and removed duplicate rows to ensure unique entries.

2. Missing Value Treatment
- Replaced blank or missing values in key columns:
  - country, cast, director, rating, and duration
  - Used "NA" as a placeholder for missing data.

3. Text Standardization & Corrections
- Trimmed leading and trailing spaces in the 'title' column for consistency.

Deliverables:
- Cleaned Dataset: netflix_cleaned.xlsx
- Summary Report: This summary document.
